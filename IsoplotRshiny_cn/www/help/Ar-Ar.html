J: Irradiation constant and its analytical uncertainty (12σ%).
39/40: Measured 39Ar/40Ar-ratios.
err[39/40]: Analytical uncertainty (12σ%) of the 39Ar/40Ar-ratios.
36/40: Measured 36Ar/40Ar-ratios.
err[36/40]: Analytical uncertainty (12σ%) of the 36Ar/40Ar-ratios.
39/36: Measured 39Ar/36Ar-ratios.
err[39/36]: Analytical uncertainty (12σ%) of the 39Ar/36Ar-ratios.
40/36: Measured 40Ar/36Ar-ratios.
err[40/36]: Analytical uncertainty (12σ%) of the 40Ar/36Ar-ratios.
rho: Error correlation between the 40Ar/36Ar- and 39Ar/36Ar-ratios.
(rho): Error correlation between the 36Ar/40Ar- and 39Ar/40Ar-ratios (optional).
(39): Amount of 39Ar in each heating step, which will be normalised to form the X-axis of the age spectrum (optional).
(C): Optional variable to be used as fill colours in isochron and radial plots.
(omit): Optional variable used to hide or ignore aliquots. Aliquots marked as (lowercase) 'x' are plotted (except on KDEs and CADs) but ignored for numerical analysis; Aliquots marked as (uppercase) 'X' are removed from both plots and calculations.

IsoplotR uses the redundancy of the three ratios to calculate the correlation between their respective analytical uncertainties.
